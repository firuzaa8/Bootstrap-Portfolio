About Me

My name is Firuza Abramyan. I have recently graduated from the Penn LPS Coding Bootcamp. It was one of the most challenging experiences I ever had to face. However, I am a big believer in constant personal growth, whether it means learning something new, or doing something that is "outside of the box" and or my "comfort zone". I am a goal achiever regardless of how long it takes. Life's journey is not about how fast it takes to get to places; it is about the experiences, it is about living in the moment, concentrating on the NOW and giving it the best I can at the moment. That strategy has helped me to be at the top of my game at all times. I live therefore I learn. I might not *yet* know everything that there is to know about coding, however, with the right tools and the proper life management style, I truly believe that there are no limits to what our minds can do.
